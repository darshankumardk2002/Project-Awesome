Welcome to Asha Medicals

Asha Medicals is your trusted healthcare partner in Attakulangara. We provide a wide range of medications, health products, and expert advice to meet your medical needs. Our dedicated team is committed to ensuring your health and well-being by offering high-quality products and personalized service. Whether you need prescription medications, over-the-counter remedies, or wellness products, Asha Medicals is here for you.

Product Categories

  • Over-the-Counter Medications:
    • Pain Relievers: Paracetamol, ibuprofen, aspirin for pain relief and fever reduction.
    • Cold and Flu Remedies: Antihistamines (cetirizine), decongestants (pseudoephedrine), cough syrups.
    • Allergy Medications: Antihistamines such as loratadine and diphenhydramine.
  • Prescription Medications:
    • Heart Medications: Beta-blockers (metoprolol), ACE inhibitors (enalapril), statins (atorvastatin).
    • Antibiotics: Amoxicillin, ciprofloxacin, doxycycline for bacterial infections.
    • Diabetes Care Products: Insulin injections, metformin, glipizide for blood sugar management.
  • First Aid Supplies:
    • Bandages: Various types of wound dressings.
    • Antiseptics: Hydrogen peroxide, iodine for cleaning wounds.
    • Wound Care Products: Sterile dressings, gauze pads, adhesive tapes.
  • Health and Wellness:
    • Vitamins: Multivitamins, vitamin C, vitamin D supplements.
    • Dietary Supplements: Omega-3 fatty acids, protein powders, probiotics.
    • Personal Care Items: Skin care lotions, shampoos, oral hygiene products.
  • Emergency Medicines:
    • Epinephrine (Epipen): For severe allergic reactions (anaphylaxis).
    • Aspirin: Used during a heart attack to reduce blood clotting.
    • Nitroglycerin: For relief of chest pain (angina).
    • Salbutamol (Inhaler): For asthma attacks and breathing difficulties.
    • Atropine: For poisoning or bradycardia (slow heart rate).
    • Adrenaline: For cardiac arrest or severe allergic reactions.
    • Diazepam: For controlling seizures and severe anxiety.
    • Naloxone: To reverse opioid overdoses.
    • Activated Charcoal: For treating certain types of poisoning.

Note: Prescription medications are available ONLY with a valid Doctor's Prescription.
